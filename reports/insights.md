# Sales Analytics Dashboard - Insights Summary

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive sales analytics dashboard analyzes **$254.1M in revenue** from **10,000 transactions** across **5 products** and **5 regions** over a **2-year period (2022-2023)**. The analysis reveals significant growth opportunities and performance patterns that can drive strategic business decisions.

## Key Performance Indicators

- **Total Revenue**: $254,118,102
- **Total Transactions**: 10,000
- **Average Order Value**: $25,412
- **Total Quantity Sold**: 502,030 units
- **Year-over-Year Growth**: 3.5% (2022 to 2023)
- **Geographic Coverage**: 5 regions (North, South, East, West, Central)
- **Product Portfolio**: 5 products (A, B, C, D, E)
